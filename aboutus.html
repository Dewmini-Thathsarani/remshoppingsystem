About Us Page

A new chapter in the Sri Lankan Fitness Industry started as iorncity gymnasium opened its doors to the public. iorncity gym aims to inspire active living, and give you access to credible health and fitness resources and experiences all in one comprehensive platform. We collaborate with professionals across the network to curate experiences, corporate seminars, and inspirational content as well.

This site provides you the most effective workout packages according to your requirement and this is the best virtual training platform in Sri Lanka. Explore the site for activities and events that may interest you, search for classes, new experiences, find healthy diets and much more!
